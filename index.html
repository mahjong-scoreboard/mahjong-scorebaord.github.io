Privacy Policy

The short version: We don't want your data, and we don't have it.

This application is designed to be completely offline. Your data never leaves your device unless you manually choose to share it with someone standing right next to you.

  • No Cloud Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage stats to any servers.
  • Local Storage: All app data is stored locally on your device.

Score Sharing & Local Connectivity

The score sharing feature uses Local WiFi Direct. This allows you to share your achievements with nearby friends without an internet connection.

  • Peer-to-Peer Only: Data is sent directly from one device to another; it never passes through the internet or our servers.
  • User-Initiated: Sharing only happens when you explicitly trigger it. The app will never share your score automatically.

Your privacy is protected by design. If you delete the app, all associated data is removed from your device.
